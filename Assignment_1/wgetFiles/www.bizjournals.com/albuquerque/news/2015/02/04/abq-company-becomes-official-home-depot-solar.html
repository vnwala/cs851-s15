Consolidated Solar Technologies, an Albuquerque-based solar-power integrator, has become the sole supplier of solar service to Home Depot stores throughout the region.

Email

Consolidated Solar Technologies, an Albuquerque-based solar-power supply and installation company, has become the sole supplier of solar service to Home Depot stores throughout the region.

CST is now providing all solar supplies for Home Depot in New Mexico, southwest Colorado and western Arizona. The company currently has solar displays at all Home Depot locations in Albuquerque, Rio Rancho, Los Lunas, Santa Fe, Gallup, Farmington and Durango. They will soon add solar displays at Home Depots in Arizona, including Yuma, Lake Havasu, Bullhead City, Kingman, Sierra Vista, Thatcher, Nogales, Show Low and Payson.

"This is a very exciting expansion for our company," explained Tom Styer, CST's General Manager. "We will be continuing our partnership with Home Depot as we expand further west and into applicable Home Depot markets. Customers can have peace of mind knowing that a successful, national company endorses our service and products."
