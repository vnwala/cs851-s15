RAISE SOME HAIRWARENESS!

It's hard for people to participate in or donate to a cause they don't know about, so start by spreading the word!

Talk the Talk. Tell friends, family, even the guy at the bus stop, absolutely EVERYBODY why they should shave their heads to support cancer research.

Got a Facebook Page? Got more than one fan? Good! Start spreading the word on how they can contribute to the cause. If you're shaving, post your #NoHairSelfie on your page!

Do you tweet? Sweet, then tweet this: I'm shaving my head for cancer research-sponsor me! #NoHairSelfie #WorldCancerDay nohairselfie.com

App-ply yourself! Download the #NoHairSelfie App and be a virtual crop top!

A picture is worth a thousand words! Post your #NoHairSelfie on Pinterest and get others to join in!

Dare everyone to shave their heads by tweeting, facebooking, texting, emailing and just plain telling people every chance you get!

Share your #NoHairSelfie Profile Page across all your social media networks!

If you belong to a gym or a religious or cultural organization, get people there involved too. The more who know, the greater the awareness!
